General description:
400 Must-Have Words for TOEFL is specially designed to help you master the core vocabulary and basic written and spoken language skills required to do well on the TOEFL and pursue a course of study at a North American school, or a school with an English-language curriculum. Based on the authors' years of experience as ESL teachers and their rigorous review of past exams, this book offers proven vocabulary-building activities and exercises. Appropriate for students pursuing all academic disciplines, it features:400 words in key categories, including food, computers, society, history, finance, politics, friendship, science, the arts, and more Many skill-building activities and exercises, including paraphrasing, synonym and antonym matching, and prefix and suffix identification Proven strategies for incorporating new words into everyday vocabulary Special sections on synonyms, roots, prefixes and suffixes, and sentence structure

Our experience:
Even though the book is primarily created for TOEFL preparation, it forms an excellent foundation for any advanced-level English exam, including the SAT. Roughly said, this book helps you take your English from level B2 to solid B2+. Concerning both English and math, we generally advise you first to improve your theoretical knowledge and then start working on the test exercises - your practice will yield much more effective results with a stronger foundation. Therefore, try to work on this book at the beginning of the preparation process. The rich vocabulary thus gained will be very helpful for the Reading Section of the SAT, which is flooded with complex words that are often unfamiliar to a non-native speaker. If you take most of this book, you will be able to understand reading passages well; however, 400 Must-Have Words for TOEFL represents the second foundational book in our vocabulary series. In order to maximize your scores, consider working on 504 Absolutely Essential Words, 1100 Words You Need to Know, etc.
