General description:
This book has long been a favorite among students and teachers because it adds a large element of fun to vocabulary-building exercises. It contains 40 lessons with word lists, definitions, pronunciations, and fascinating information on word origins and changing usage. Eexcellent preparation for standardized verbal and language tests, including PSAT, SAT, and ACT.

Our experience:
601 Words You Need to Know to Pass Your Exam is the last book from our vocabulary series. We would recommend this book if you are aiming to score 750+ on the English Section of the SAT. Roughly said, the vocabulary is selected from language level C1+ and focuses on complex words that are not very frequently used in a single exam. Therefore, if your target score is not 750+, this book may not be worth your time. But if you decide on it, take it after you complete all the lower-level options you have.
