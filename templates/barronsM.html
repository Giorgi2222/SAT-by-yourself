General description:
Barron’s Math Workbook for the NEW SAT reflects all of the new questions and question types that will appear on the new SAT. Students will discover:
- Hundreds of revised math questions with answer explanations
- Math strategies to help test-takers approach and correctly answer all of the question types on the SAT
- All questions answered and explained

Here is an intensive preparation for the SAT's all-important Math section, and a valuable learning tool for college-bound students who need extra help in math and feel the need to raise their math scores.


Our experience:
Math Workbook for the New SAT is a book from one of the most popular publishers - Barron’s - and is comparable to Math Workout for the SAT from The Princeton Review. This book is a section-specific guide for math. It includes clear instructions about every mathematical topic tested by SAT and provides plenty of exercises to help you boost your score. If you are thinking of taking College Panda’s SAT Math, we highly recommend that you practice the strategies you learned from it in this workbook too. In this case, you will ignore all the techniques and other descriptions provided here. If you have very limited time, concentrate on College Panda’s SAT Math and don’t spend time on this book. However, be aware that using this workbook will help you a lot because College Panda’s Math focuses only on the harder problems of the test, and thus you may miss some easier topics that you might be unsure of.
