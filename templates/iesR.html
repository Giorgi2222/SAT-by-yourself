General description:
Confront and conquer one of the most challenging SAT sections with this new, expert-designed workbook. Ten full New SAT Reading tests will give you the intensive practice that you need to enhance your reading comprehension and critical thinking skills. Be part of the IES record of excellence, and get ready to raise your SAT score!

Our experience:
IES New SAT Reading Practice Book provides 10 practice tests only for the reading section of the SAT. It is an amazing resource helping you to use your newly acquired skills and strategies in practice. The practice tests provided are very similar to the real structure and style of SAT; therefore, they can help you work on your pacing. SAT is one of the most ruthlessly timed exams, and developing pacing skills is crucial for success. We provided some full test options, and they will help your timing too, but we recommend that you complete these tests first. Even though they are structurally very similar to real SAT, they are a little bit easier. Therefore, if you start testing yourself with these and then get to the harder ones, your transition will be much more smooth and effective.
