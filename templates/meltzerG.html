General description:
"The Ultimate Guide to SAT® Grammar" provides comprehensive coverage of all the grammar, rhetoric, and data-analysis concepts tested on the redesigned SAT® Writing and Language Test, and includes more than 300 new in-depth explanations to practice questions. Dozens of exercises help students move from studying concepts individually to applying their skills in a passage-based context. Includes a complete index of Writing questions from the eight exams in the College Board Official SAT Study Guide, 2020 Edition, arranged both by category and by test.

Our experience:
The Ultimate Guide to SAT Grammar is a book by a remarkable author. Erica Meltzer is an experienced SAT tutor; all her books are amazingly efficient. This book focuses on the grammar needed specifically for the SAT and apart from theory, introduces you to the real questions from SAT tests. If you have limited time, we recommend starting prep with Ultimate Guide to SAT Grammar and complementing it with the Blue Book whenever you discover any grammatical topics that you are not good at. This book offers you various strategies for approaching the SAT tests; therefore, while working on it, make sure you do Writing Section practice tests too in order to employ the techniques you learned and determine which ones work for you.
